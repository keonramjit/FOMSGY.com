The Cost of Disconnected Operations

Regional carriers face critical bottlenecks that compound into financial and safety risks.

Data Silos

Dispatchers use spreadsheets, pilots use paper logs. Critical info is scattered.

Manual Errors

Re-typing schedules from PDFs leads to high-risk data entry mistakes.

Compliance Gaps

Missed FDP limits and license expiries due to lack of automated oversight.

Latency

Communication lags between dispatch and crew affect turnaround times.
